Entity colors

These colors are used for coloring entities. Colors for polymer and branched entities are drawn from the beginning of the sequence, colors for non-polymer entities (ligands) are drawn from the end (more bleached colors that will not conflict with chemical element coloring). Grays are avoided on purpose.
